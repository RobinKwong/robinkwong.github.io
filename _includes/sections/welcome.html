This is a website about the intersection of journalism and technology … plus a third and most imporpant aspect: people.

I am talking about the people who make their living producing the news, at that intersection. Specifically, I am interested in how they organise themselves and how they are managed. This is a website where I will be writing about just that. A good place to start would be my post on why newsrooms need project managers.
